# Asset Types

Assets in the Odude ecosystem are categorized into four main types, each serving a specific purpose and offering unique features for both the issuer and the recipient.

## 1. Badge
Badges are used for social recognition, achievements, and membership status.
- **Uses**: Community milestones, "Early Adopter" status, or contribution awards.
- **Display**: Typically displayed as small icons on the user's profile card or sidebar.
- **Value**: Establishes social proof and reputation within the platform.

## 2. Certificate
Certificates represent official verification of skills, completion of courses, or formal credentials.
- **Uses**: Course completion, identity verification, or professional endorsements.
- **Features**: Often contains metadata like "Issue Date" and "Expiry Date."
- **Display**: Shown as a formal document or a verified badge on the profile.

## 3. Ticket
Tickets provide access rights to events, digital content, or physical venues.
- **Uses**: Webinar access, VIP area entry, or physical event check-ins.
- **Features**: Can be "scanned" or "validated" by designated agents.
- **Display**: Usually features a QR code or a unique access key when viewed in detail.

## 4. Coupon
Coupons are promotional assets that offer discounts or special deals.
- **Uses**: 20% off at the Marketplace, free trial keys, or partner discounts.
- **Features**: Includes "Redeem" logic and expiration tracking.
- **Display**: Shows the discount value and a "Redeem" button for the user.
