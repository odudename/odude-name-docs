# About Assets

Digital assets on Odude represent social proof, achievements, or access rights that are issued to users. Unlike standard marketplace items, these assets are often minted by authorized owners and received by users to enhance their digital profile.

![Assets Gallery](/assets/docs/assets/asset_gallery.png)

## Overview
Assets are managed in two primary locations depending on your role:
- **Receivers**: Manage received assets via the [Asset Management](/docs/assets/management) page (`name.odude.com/assets`).
- **Owners (Issuers)**: Create and issue assets via the [Owner Dashboard](/docs/assets/owner) (`name.odude.com/owner`).

## Who Can Create Assets?
Creating and issuing assets is a privileged action. While any user can receive an asset, only users who have been granted **Special Rights** by the administrator can access the Owner Dashboard to create new assets.

## Core Asset Types
The ecosystem supports several distinct types of assets including:
- **Badges**: For social recognition and achievements.
- **Certificates**: For official verification and credentials.
- **Tickets**: For event access and entry.
- **Coupons**: For discounts and promotional offers.
