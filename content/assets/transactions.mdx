# Transaction History

The Transaction History page (`name.odude.com/owner/transaction`) provides a detailed log of every asset issuance and transfer initiated from your owner account. It is the primary tool for auditing and managing the distribution of your assets.

## Search and Filtering
Managing a large number of transactions is made easy with robust tools:
- **Search**: Search for specific transactions by **Recipient Name** or **Asset Title**.
- **Filters**: Filter the history by **Asset Type** (Badge, Certificate, etc.) or by the **Status** of the transfer.

## Transfer Statuses
Each transaction in your history will have a specific status indicating its current state:

| Status | Meaning |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Pending** | The asset has been sent but the recipient has not yet taken action. |
| **Approved** | The recipient has accepted the asset and it is now on their profile. |
| **Declined** | The recipient has rejected the asset. |
| **Revoked** | The owner has cancelled the transfer before it was accepted or pulled it back. |

## Revoking a Transfer
If you have issued an asset by mistake or need to cancel a pending issuance, you can use the **Revoke** feature.
- **How to**: Locate the transaction in the list (usually in Pending status) and click the **Revoke** button.
- **Effect**: The asset will be removed from the recipient's Pending list and they will no longer be able to accept it.
