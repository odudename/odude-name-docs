# Owner Dashboard

The Owner Dashboard (`name.odude.com/owner`) is a specialized interface for users authorized to issue and manage assets at scale. 

> **Access Note**: This dashboard is only visible and accessible to users who have been granted **Special Rights** by the platform administrator. If you do not have these rights, the page will not be accessible.

## Dashboard Overview
The dashboard is split into two primary sections to manage your identity as an issuer and your assets.

### Profile Tab
In this tab, you manage your "Issuer Identity." This is how you appear to users when they receive an asset from you.
- **Brand Identity**: Set your issuer name, logo, and description.
- **Verification**: Display your official status to build trust with asset recipients.

### Assets Tab
This is the command center for asset creation and issuance.

![Asset Owner Dashboard](/assets/docs/assets/asset_dashboard.png)



#### How to Create an Asset
1.  **Define Properties**: Click the "Create New Asset" button. You will need to provide a title, description, and upload the asset image (e.g., the badge icon or certificate design).
2.  **Select Type**: Choose from the available types: Badge, Certificate, Ticket, or Coupon.
3.  **Set Attributes**: Add specific metadata like "Expiry Date" for certificates or "Redemption Value" for coupons.
4.  **Confirm and Mint**: Review the details and the associated cost before finalizing.

![Asset Creation](/assets/docs/assets/asset_creation.png)


### Issue Asset
Once an asset is created, you can distribute it to specific Odude Names.
- **Individual Issue**: Enter a single Odude Name to send the asset.

![Asset Issue](/assets/docs/assets/asset_issue.png)
