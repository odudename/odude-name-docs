# Manage Credits

Managing your ODude Credits is simple and transparent. You can track every transaction and easily share credits with other users in the ecosystem.

## Credit History
Every time you earn or spend credits, a record is created in your history. This allows you to maintain a clear audit trail of your digital currency.

### How to View History
1. Navigate to your **Credits Dashboard**.
2. Click on the **History** or **Transaction Log** section.
3. View the list of all activities with timestamps, descriptions, and credit amounts.

![Credit History](/assets/docs/credits/credit_history.png)

## Transferring Credits
You can transfer your ODude Credits to any other user who has an active ODude Name.

### Steps to Transfer
1. On your Credits dashboard, locate and click the **Transfer Credits** button.
2. Enter the **Recipient's ODude Name** (e.g., `friend@odude`).
3. Enter the **Amount** of credits you wish to transfer.
4. (Optional) Add a note for the recipient.
5. Click **Confirm Transfer** to complete the transaction.

![Transfer Credits](/assets/docs/credits/credit_transfer.png)

> **Note:** Credits can only be transferred to active ODude Name accounts. Always double-check the recipient's name before confirming.
