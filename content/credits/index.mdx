# ODude Credits

ODude Credits are the digital reward system integrated across the ODude ecosystem. They are designed to incentivize engagement, reward participation, and manage internal costs within the platform.

## Overview
Credits act as a digital currency within the ODude platform. You can use them to unlock features, create new digital identities, and participate in the ODude Marketplace.

## How to Earn Credits
Users are rewarded for various actions within the ecosystem:
- **Account Creation**: New users receive welcome credits upon signing up for an ODude Name.
- **Marketplace Sales**: Earn credits by selling products or services on the ODude Marketplace.
- **Platform Engagement**: Regular use and social interactions can earn you additional rewards.
- **Referrals**: Inviting new users to join the ODude community.

## How to Spend Credits
Credits can be used for several key functions:
- **ODude Names**: Used as the fee structure for generating new digital names and unlocking premium features.
- **Marketplace Purchases**: Buy products and services from other users using your credits.
- **Service Renewals**: Extend or manage your digital identities.

## Tracking Your Credits
Your current credit balance is always visible in the sidebar of your dashboard. For a detailed breakdown of your earnings and spending, you can access your transaction history.

## Social Rewards & Future Benefits
The ODude ecosystem is constantly evolving to reward its most active participants.
- **Bookmarks**: Future updates may include rewards based on how many times your ODude Name has been bookmarked by other users.
- **Participation**: Active members of the community will be the first to receive new types of digital rewards and platform bonuses.
