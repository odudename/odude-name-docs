# Introduction to Odude

Welcome to the Odude ecosystem. Odude is a comprehensive platform designed to provide unique digital identities and a marketplace for digital assets.

## Core Components

### Names
Your identity in the Odude system begins with a unique Name (e.g., `user@odude`). This name serves as your universal identifier across the ecosystem.

### Marketplace
The Odude Marketplace is where users can trade, buy, and sell digital assets. It connects creators and collectors in a secure environment.

### Points
Engage with the platform to earn Points. These points can be used for various activities within the Odude system, rewarding your participation.

### Assets & Bookmarks
Manage your digital heritage and keep track of what matters most to you.
