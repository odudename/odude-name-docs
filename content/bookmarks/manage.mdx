# Managing Bookmarks

Learning how to manage your bookmarks will help you get the most out of the ODude ecosystem.

## How to Bookmark an ODude Name

Bookmarking is simple and can be done from any profile card you encounter while browsing:

1. Locate the **Bookmark Icon** (usually a bookmark or ribbon symbol) on the profile card.
2. Click the icon.
3. You will see a notification confirming the bookmark.
4. The icon will change color (usually to yellow/gold) to indicate it is now bookmarked.
5. You will see a "Flying Points" animation indicating you have earned **10 points**.

![How to Bookmark](/assets/docs/bookmarks/bookmark_ribbon.png)

## Viewing your Bookmark List

To see all the names you have bookmarked:

1. Go to the **Main Sidebar** or **Navigation Menu**.
2. Select the **Bookmarks** option.
3. This will open a page displaying all your saved names in a grid.
4. You can search through your bookmarks using the search bar at the top of the page.

![Bookmark Page](/assets/docs/bookmarks/bookmark_list.png)

## Removing a Bookmark

If you no longer wish to follow a name or keep it in your list:

1. Locate the profile in your **Bookmarks** list or on its public profile.
2. Click the **Bookmark Icon** again.
3. The icon will return to its original state (gray/hollow).
4. **Important:** 10 points will be deducted from your balance, and 10 points will be deducted from the owner's balance as well.

## Managing Bookmarked Contacts

From the Bookmark page, you can:
- **Visit Profiles:** Click on any bookmarked card to go directly to that user's full profile.
- **Support Creators:** By keeping a bookmark active, you help the creator maintain their point balance.
- **Clean up:** Regularly review your bookmarks to keep your list organized and relevant to your interests.
