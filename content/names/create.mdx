# Registering a Name

Creating your unique identity on Odude is a straightforward process. This guide provides a step-by-step walkthrough to help you secure and set up your first Odude Name.

## Step 1: Navigating to the Registration Page

To begin registration, navigate to the **Names** section on the main Odude portal dashboard.

> **Note**: You must be logged in to your Odude account to register a name.

![Navigate to Names section]
*(Instruction: Replace this image with a screenshot showing the 'Names' link in the sidebar or dashboard.)*

## Step 2: Searching for Availability

In the search field, enter your desired handle (e.g., `satoshi`). The system will instantly check if the name is available for registration.

- **Available**: If the name is free, you will see a "Register" button.
- **Unavailable**: If the name is already taken, try adding numbers or unique characters to make it distinct.

![Search for handle availability]
*(Instruction: Replace this image with a screenshot of the search bar showing a result for an available name.)*

## Step 3: Completing the Registration

Click the **Register** button next to your chosen name. You may be asked to confirm the registration or provide additional details depending on the current platform requirements.

Once registered, you can immediately start adding records like your display name, bio, and social links.
