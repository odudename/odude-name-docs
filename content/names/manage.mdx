# Managing Your Names

Once you have registered an Odude Name, you can manage it through the **My Names** dashboard. This guide explains how to update your profile records, transfer your name to someone else, or delete it.

## Updating Profile Records

Your Odude Name acts as a container for various records. You can update these at any time to keep your profile current.

1.  Select the name you want to edit from your list.
2.  Click on the **Edit** or **Settings** icon.
3.  Update fields such as:
    - **Display Name**: Your preferred public name.
    - **Description/Bio**: A short intro about yourself.
    - **Avatar**: A URL to your profile picture.
    - **Social Links**: Links to your Twitter, GitHub, Website, etc.
4.  Click **Save Changes**.

## Transferring Ownership

You can securely transfer ownership of an Odude Name to another user's address.

> **Warning**: This action is irreversible once confirmed. The name will no longer be under your control.

1.  Navigate to the **Transfer** tab within the management page for your name.
2.  Enter the recipient's wallet address or Odude account ID.
3.  Review the details and click **Confirm Transfer**.

## Deleting a Name

If you no longer wish to keep a name, you can delete it. This will release the name back into the public pool for others to register.

1.  Navigate to the **Settings** tab for your name.
2.  Find the "Danger Zone" and click **Delete Name**.
3.  Type the name to confirm deletion.
