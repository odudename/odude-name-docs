# Odude Names

Odude Names are the cornerstone of your identity within the Odude platform. Unlike traditional usernames, an Odude Name is designed to be a portable and distinct handle that you own.

## Why get an Odude Name?
- **Uniqueness**: Secure a name that represents you.
- **Identity**: Use it across the entire Odude ecosystem.
- **Ownership**: You have full control over your name and its associated records.
