# Introduction to Marketplace

The Odude Marketplace is a platform where you can list, sell, and buy digital assets and services. It provides a secure environment for creators and sellers to monetize their offerings while allowing buyers to discover and purchase unique items.

## Overview
Whether you are a seller looking to reach a wider audience or a buyer searching for specific digital goods, the Odude Marketplace offers the tools you need to manage transactions effectively.

### Key Features
- **Seller Tools**: Manage your products and track your sales with ease.
- **Order Management**: Keep track of your purchases and sales through a dedicated dashboard.
- **Secure Transactions**: Built-in mechanisms to ensure safe trading between users.

> **Note**: The marketplace is currently accessible through individual product links and your personal dashboard. The global `/market` discovery page is currently being updated.
