# Order Management

All transactions on the Odude Marketplace can be tracked and managed via the Orders page. This page serves both buyers and sellers, providing a transparent view of every booking and purchase.

## How to order a product

1. Go to the product page
2. Click on the "Buy" button
3. Follow the prompts to complete the purchase

![Buyer Order Management Placeholder](/assets/docs/marketplace/product_booking.png)


You can access your orders at: `https://name.odude.com/market/orders`

## For Buyers: Managing Your Purchases

When you buy or book a product, it appears in your **Purchases/My Orders** section.
- **Order Status**: Check if your order is "Pending", "Processing", or "Completed".
- **Access Digital Goods**: Once an order is marked as completed, you can often find the download links or access codes right here.
- **Contact Seller**: Reach out to the seller directly if you have questions about your order.
- **Receipts**: View and download details for your records.

![Buyer Order Management Placeholder](/assets/docs/marketplace/my_order.png)

## For Sellers: Managing Your Sales

As a seller, the Orders page is where you fulfill your commitments.
- **New Bookings**: Get notified of new orders that require your attention.
- **Fulfillment**: Update the status of an order to let the buyer know you are working on it or have delivered the item.
- **Order History**: Keep a record of all past sales for tax and reporting purposes.
- **Buyer Information**: Securely view the details provided by the buyer to complete the delivery.

![Seller Order Management Placeholder](/assets/docs/marketplace/seller_dashboard_order.png) 

---

## Order Status Workflow
1. **Created/Pending**: The buyer has initiated the order but payment or confirmation is pending.
2. **Paid/Processing**: Payment is confirmed, and the seller should prepare the digital asset.
3. **Completed**: The asset has been delivered, and the transaction is closed.
4. **Cancelled**: The order was terminated by either party.
