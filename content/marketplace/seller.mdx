# Seller Dashboard

The Seller Dashboard is your central command center for managing your digital business on Odude. From here, you can oversee your inventory and keep track of incoming orders.

## Products Tab

The **Products** tab is where you manage all your listings. Here, you can:
- **View Listings**: See all the products you have created, their current status (Active/Inactive), and prices.
- **Edit Products**: Update product details, pricing, or descriptions at any time.
- **Delete/Archive**: Remove products that are no longer available for sale.
- **Add New**: Quickly navigate to the creation page to list a new digital asset.

## Orders Tab

The **Orders** tab allows you to monitor all sales transactions. This section is crucial for fulfillment and customer service.
- **Pending Orders**: View orders that are currently in progress or awaiting action.
- **Completed Sales**: Access a history of all successfully completed transactions.
- **Order Details**: Click on any order to see specific buyer information, delivery status, and timestamps.
- **Status Updates**: Change the status of an order as you process it (e.g., from Pending to Shipped/Delivered).
