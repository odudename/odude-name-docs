# Creating a Product

Listing a new product on the Odude Marketplace is straightforward. This guide explains all the fields required and how your product will be presented to potential buyers.

## Product Fields

When creating a new listing, you will encounter the following fields:

![Product Creation Placeholder](/assets/docs/marketplace/product_creation.png)

### 1. Title
The name of your product. This should be clear and descriptive to attract buyers.

### 2. Description
Explain what your product is, what is included, and any special instructions for the buyer. You can use markdown to format this text.

### 3. Price
Set the price for your product. Ensure it reflects the value of the digital asset.

### 4. Category
Choose the most relevant category to help buyers find your product through filters.

### 5. Media/Images
Upload high-quality images or videos that showcase your product. First image usually serves as the cover.

### 6. Digital File/URL
Provide the actual digital asset or a secure link where the buyer can access the product after purchase.

## How it looks to End Users

Once your product is published, it is assigned a unique URL. This page is optimized for conversion, clearly displaying all relevant information to the buyer.

### Example Product Page
You can see a live example of a product page here:
[View Example Product](https://name.odude.com/market/HyiFzKt2zvYj)

**Key features of the public product page:**
- **Prominent Buy Button**: Makes it easy for users to initiate a purchase.
- **Product Gallery**: Sliders or grids to view multiple images of the asset.
- **Seller Profile**: Links to your Odude profile so buyers can see your reputation.
- **Detailed Description**: All the info you provided in the creation step, formatted for readability.

![Product Page Preview Placeholder](/assets/docs/marketplace/product_page.png)

## Requirements
To create a product, you must:
- Have a verified Odude account.
- Have a valid payment method linked (if applicable for payouts).
- Ensure your digital asset complies with our terms of service.
